Some interesting facts about Moray Eels include...

They have 2 sets of jaws
  • They attack there victims with a hidden set of teeth for extra lethality
  • Outer Jaws grasp the target while the inner set of teeth pulls the prey down its throat
Roughly 200 species
  • Some species like the Zebra Moray have blunt teeth for dining on hard shell prey such as crabs, clams, mollusks, etc
They are nocturnal
  • These Eels happen to be nocturnal meaning they are active when the sun sets and take night time swims
