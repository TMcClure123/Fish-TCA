Some interesting facts about Otters include...

There are 12 species of Otter still alive today
  • Asian small-clawed Otter
  • Smooth-coated Otter
  • Hairy-nosed Otter
  • Spotted-neck Otter
  • African Clawless Otter
  • Congo Clawless Otter
  • Eurasian Otter
  • Cape Clawless Otter
  • American River Otter
  • Southern River Otter
  • Giant Otter
  • Sea Otter
Otters can Smell Underwater
  • Otters have a unique sense of smell which allows them to smell underwater without having the risk of inhailing water and potentially dying
Otters use Tools
  • Otters acctually sometimes are seen using rocks to smash open there prey such as molluscs and crabs
